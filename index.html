Project: RiderCam

Delivery Riders Deserve Protection, Not Just "Gigs."

We are building the first safety camera system designed specifically for gig workers. No expensive hardware costs. Just protection.

Built for Delivery Riders

The Problem We Solve

Delivery riders face unique risks every day:

  • False "non-delivery" complaints that get your account deactivated
  • Accidents with no proof of what happened
  • Theft of your earnings and gear
  • Apps that don't have your back when disputes arise

Why RiderCam Is Different

Unlike action cameras designed for sports, RiderCam is built specifically for the realities of delivery work:

  • Continuous loop recording that never runs out of space
  • Soldered encrypted storage - no SD cards to lose or manage
  • Event tagging button - one press protects important footage
  • IP65 water-resistant - built for rain and weather
  • Quick-release mounting - attaches to chest or dashboard

How It Works

1. Mount - Quick-release rail system attaches to your chest or dashboard
2. Record - Continuous capture of your entire shift with GPS data
3. Tag Events - Press the button to protect important moments from overwriting
4. Sync - Automatically uploads to cloud when connected to home WiFi

Our Pricing Model

We believe safety shouldn't cost $400 upfront. We use a Hardware-as-a-Service model:

  • $0 hardware cost - just a nominal setup fee
  • $14.99/month subscription for full access
  • No contract - cancel anytime, 14-day device return period

Protection shouldn't be a luxury. We're making it affordable for every delivery rider.

Smart Features

  • Haptic feedback - vibrates when recording starts or if something goes wrong
  • Status LEDs - clear visual indicators for power, recording, and errors
  • Automatic thermal management - keeps recording in any temperature
  • 4-hour battery - replaceable battery for longer shifts

Why We Are Building This

False complaints. Accidents. Theft. The apps don't have your back, and GoPros cost $400. We believe safety shouldn't be a luxury item.

The Concept

We want to eliminate the upfront cost of safety. Our goal is to provide a specialized camera for $0 upfront, supported by a simple app membership.

The Technology

We are designing a camera that mounts to your chest or dashboard, records to a secure black box, and syncs automatically. No SD cards to manage.
