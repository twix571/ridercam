Project: RiderCam

Delivery Riders Deserve Protection, Not Just "Gigs."

We are building the first safety camera system designed specifically for gig workers. No expensive hardware costs. Just protection.

Why We Are Building This

False complaints. Accidents. Theft. The apps don't have your back, and GoPros cost $400. We believe safety shouldn't be a luxury item.

The Concept

We want to eliminate the upfront cost of safety. Our goal is to provide a specialized camera for $0 upfront, supported by a simple app membership.

The Technology

We are designing a camera that mounts to your chest or dashboard, records to a secure black box, and syncs automatically. No SD cards to manage.

Current Status: Development

We want to be transparent: We are currently in the Engineering Phase. We are not selling anything yet. We are building a community of riders to help us get the features right.
